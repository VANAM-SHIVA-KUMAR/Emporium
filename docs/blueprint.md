# **App Name**: Ramdev Emporium Online

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse and search the full catalog of lighting products including LED lights, chandeliers, and decorative lights.
- Product Detail Pages: View detailed specifications, images, and customer reviews for each product.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Suggest relevant lighting products to customers based on their browsing history and purchase behavior. The AI tool learns preferences.
- Shopping Cart and Checkout: Add products to a shopping cart and complete the purchase through a secure checkout process.
- Customer Accounts: Allow customers to create accounts to save their favorite products, track order history, and manage their profile information.
- Contact Information: Display Ramdev Emporium's address (Wrya Khammam Road, Khammam) and contact details prominently.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm red (#D32F2F) inspired by the company's storefront signage, evoking a sense of tradition and reliability.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) provides a clean and neutral backdrop for the products.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFC107) used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and special offers, to add a touch of luxury.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif font to create a sophisticated and fashionable high-end feel
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif font to ensure a modern and easy to read experience.
- Use clear and modern icons to represent product categories and website navigation elements.
- A clean and intuitive layout with a prominent search bar and easy navigation to different product categories.
